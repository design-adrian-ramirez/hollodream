2 PIECE

Rooted in the energy of Miami’s nightlife, Tampa-born DJ and producer 2 PIECE fuses tech house with Latin and old-school influences. His sets are raw, groovy, and crafted to move the dance floor. Guided by the belief that “Everyone is equal on the dance floor,” he creates inclusive, high-energy experiences that bring people together through music.
