ELIDERP

Fort Worth–based producer eliderp has quickly risen through the bass scene with his boundary-pushing blend of dubstep and neuro-driven sound design. Since debuting in 2018, he’s released on powerhouse labels including Monstercat, Cyclops Recordings, Disciple, and Halcyon, earning recognition for his technical production and forward-thinking style. In 2024, eliderp made his Monstercat debut on Mr. Bill’s Mechanomorphic LP, contributing the standout tracks Big Chordi Boi and Barely See (with The Widdler). Backed by collaborations with leading names and releases across top-tier imprints, eliderp is cementing his place as one of bass music’s bold new voices.
