WAKEUP

Phoenix, Arizona native “wakeup” is a DJ and producer of over 10 years, specializing in underground electronic sounds, rowdy party sets, and remixes of classic tunes. Getting his start at local queer bars and underground raves, wakeup lives to make people dance and enjoy the life they were given. He strives to spread the love of life found at on the dance floor in everything he does.
